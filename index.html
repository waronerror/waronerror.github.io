Nov. 27, 2016 - Premise

There is now a war between fact and fiction posing as fact. Good decisions require good and complete information. Similarly, errors in information produce errors in decisions. Misinformation is being used as a weapon to confuse, mislead, and manipulate our citizenry. The proliferation of misinformation is a critical threat to a peaceful, prosperous, and fair society. Although viewpoints differ, uniting around the minimization of error in information throughout our discourse should be a given. This is an evolving record of our corrections to misinformation, our dissemination of cogent arguments, and our editorial opinions.
